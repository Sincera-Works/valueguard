ValueGuard Configs

Portable, signed content-filter policies for the on-device macOS filter.

ValueGuard samples your screen at 1 Hz and runs SigLIP-2 on the Apple Neural Engine, comparing each frame against a policy compiled from a plain-English values statement. Pixels never leave your device. This registry hosts only configs — JSON, signed metadata, and calibrated float vectors; it never sees your screen content. Every bundle is signed with Ed25519, and vg install verifies the signature offline before activating it.
