# Corporate acceptable-use policy

On corporate laptops, the following content categories are not allowed
during work hours:

- Adult or sexually explicit material of any kind.
- Online gambling websites, including sports betting and online casinos.
- Streaming entertainment video (Netflix, Hulu, YouTube watch pages, Twitch
  live streams). YouTube tutorials and documentation videos are allowed.
- Social media feeds (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, X/Twitter timeline).
  LinkedIn is allowed because it is used for professional research.

All flagged events should be logged locally for the user's own review.
Nothing should be transmitted off-device. The audit log is for the
employee, not the company.

Hard blocks are reserved for explicit adult content and gambling. Other
categories should log only.
