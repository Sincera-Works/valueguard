# Personal values

I can watch movies and shows with violence, including bloody action scenes,
because that is part of mainstream entertainment I want to keep enjoying.

I do not want to see explicit sexual nudity or sexual acts in any medium —
photographs, illustrations, 3D renders, screenshots of adult sites, stills
from adult films. This includes anything that would clearly belong on an
adult website.

I am okay with art-historical or educational nudity (classical paintings,
medical textbook diagrams) but not with photographic or contemporary
illustrated nudity that is sexualised in intent.

I do not want to land on gambling websites or online casino interfaces.
Sports betting interfaces are also included.

I want to be flagged but not blocked for graphic real-world violence (news
footage of war, accidents, executions). I want to know I'm looking at it
but I do not want the screen to be ripped away from me — there are times
I will choose to engage with it deliberately (news, documentaries).
